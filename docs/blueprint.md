# **App Name**: Krav Manager

## Core Features:

- Student Onboarding Form: Generate a shareable link for new students to fill in their basic details: name, date of birth, CPF, clothing sizes, contact info, and emergency contacts.
- Detailed Student Profiles: Expand the initial student data with internal details: training start date, last grading exam date, belt color, medical history, general notes, active/inactive status, FIKM annual fee payment.
- Financial Records: Keep track of financial status
- Payment Tracking: Record plan type (monthly/quarterly), value, last payment date, plan validity, and credit notes.
- Automated Overdue Notices: Send automated payment reminders to students with expired plans via email or WhatsApp.
- Sales Tracking: Track sales of uniforms, books, supplements, and other merchandise.
- Personalized Training Recommendations: AI tool to suggest personalized training routines and drills, tailored to the student's rank, medical history, and performance data. The tool uses reasoning to decide when to recommend modifications based on individual student needs and progress.
- Monthly Reports: Generate monthly reports on payments, active students, and overdue payments.
- Import/Export Records: Enable easy import and export of student records.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#00008e) evoking a sense of authority and calm.
- Secondary color: White for clean backgrounds and content separation.
- Tertiary color: Black and shades of gray for text and interface elements, ensuring readability and a minimalist aesthetic.
- Modern, sans-serif font for body and headline, ensuring readability and a contemporary look.
- Use minimalist, vector-based icons representing actions like 'add student', 'payment', 'reminder' and 'sales'.
- Clean, tabular layouts for student data and financial records, ensuring easy scanning and information retrieval.
- Subtle transitions and animations for actions like form submissions and data updates.
